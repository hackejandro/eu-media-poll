EU Media Vibe Check

How do you see different EU media? Rate them for:

Access ↔ Adversarial

Access: journalism built on cultivating proximity to power, exclusive leaks, sympathetic framing.

Adversarial: watchdog, confrontational, investigative, holding power to account.

Highbrow ↔ Lowbrow

Highbrow: policy wonkery, dense analysis, intellectual framing.

Lowbrow: gossip, personalities, horse-race politics, drama.
